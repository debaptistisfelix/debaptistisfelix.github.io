CUSTOM VIDEO PLAYER

Anime Trailers from Netflix.

The Video Player is coded from scratch. It's possible to change the Speed Rate of the video and to turn on the Cinema Mode by toggling the input in the options.

Below there's a small Playlist of 3 Anime Trailers from Netflix with some quick infos about them. The User can browse between the videos of the playlist and choose which one to see just by clicking on the choosen trailer or by clicking the Next Button in the Video-Player controllers.

- Video-player features (Cinema Mode, Speed-rate)

- Basic Video Playlist functionality

COLOR-CHANGING MUSIC-PLAYER

My Top 5 Favourite Songs

(At the moment ...)

The Music-Player creates a canva from the Album Cover of the Song currently played and then looks through its pixels for the dominant RGB color and its Shade RGB Color.

Once the Colors are found, the Player's style and color-combo matches the one from the Album Cover, giving the User a more immersive and pleasant Music Experience.

Acquired Skills

- Basic HTML Canvas skills

- Dominant Color picking function

- HTML Audio element's Methods and Events
